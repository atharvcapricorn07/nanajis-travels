The Story Behind the Site

For as long as I can remember, Nanaji has been a storyteller. Every time he returned from a journey, he brought back more than just souvenirs—he brought back pages of notes, scribbled thoughts in old notebooks, and countless digital memos stored on his phone.

While the memories were beautiful, the organization was... a bit of a mess. His travel logs were scattered across different places, making it hard to relive the full scope of his adventures.

My name is Atharv, and I decided it was time to bridge the gap between his traditional storytelling and the digital world. I wanted to create a permanent home for these memories—a place where every destination is neatly categorized, filtered, and displayed with the respect it deserves.

This website is my way of preserving Nanaji’s legacy of exploration, turning "messy" scribbles into a curated global map for our family and the world to see.
