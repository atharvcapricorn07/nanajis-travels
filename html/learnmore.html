Why Nanaji Travels

"Traveling is my way of understanding the world—not through books, but through people, places, and shared experiences..."

Nanaji’s Travel Philosophy

  • Respect every culture and every custom.
  • Be mindful in sacred places and local traditions.
  • Try local food, but prepare for the unexpected.
  • Take photos, but don’t forget to stay present.
  • Always be early—travel is smoother without a rush.
