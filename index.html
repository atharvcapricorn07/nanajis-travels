The Travellers

Our Story

✦

Based in the vibrant city of Pune, Maharashtra, Nanaji and Nani's lives are a testament to dedication and family. After dedicated careers — Nanaji with the Ministry of Defence and Nani with the State Bank of India — they both retired at the age of sixty. Having spent decades in the diligent grind of work and the heart-centered task of raising their two daughters, their retirement marked the beginning of a new chapter.

With their daughters now settled in their own lives, Nanaji and Nani have leveraged professional tours to explore the world together. These chronicles are more than just a record of places; they are the reflections of two souls who, after a lifetime of dedication, are now savouring every moment of a journey well-deserved. This space serves as a bridge between their roots in India and their family across the globe, celebrating a journey that is as much about heritage as it is about the future.

We invite you to walk these paths with them and perhaps find a spark for your own next great adventure!

— Nanaji & Nani
